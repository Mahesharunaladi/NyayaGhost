## üèóÔ∏è Technical Innovation

### Core Components

1. **Voice Recognition**
   - Web Speech API with dialect support
   - Hindi, Bhojpuri, English (expanding to 22 languages)
   - Works offline with service workers

2. **AI RightFinder Engine**
   - Maps natural language ‚Üí legal rights
   - Combines 500+ legal provisions + 1000+ welfare schemes
   - Future: GPT-4/Gemini integration with RAG

3. **Ghost Filing System**
   - Auto-generates FIRs, RTIs, legal notices
   - SMS-based status tracking (no app required)
   - Digital signature integration planned

4. **PWA Architecture**
   - Installable, offline-capable
   - No app store barriers
   - Works on ‚Çπ2000 smartphones
